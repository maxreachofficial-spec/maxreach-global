Common Queries

Understand Our Process, Transparent Pricing, & Professional Standards

01 What is the pricing model for marketing projects at MaxReach Global? +

We follow an industry-standard 50/50 Milestone Model. Upon project approval, a 50% commitment fee is required to allot niche-specific experts to your task. The remaining 50% balance is payable only after you review and approve the final delivery.

02 Is my payment safe? (Legal Trust & Invoicing) +

Absolutely. For every transaction, we issue a Digital Project Invoice and a Service Commitment Note. These documents serve as a professional legal record of our Gurgaon-based agency operations and ensure full team accountability.

03 How is your agency work better than individual freelancers? +

MaxReach Global is a Quality-Curation Platform. Unlike generalist freelancers, we assign niche-specific experts (e.g., Real Estate, E-commerce). Our internal quality audit ensures error-free, high-performance content for your brand.

04 Why should I trust your internal project management process? +

We are a "Process-First" digital agency. Before starting, we provide a Project Scope Document (PSD) outlining exact delivery dates and quality benchmarks, ensuring every marketing commitment is delivered on time.

05 What happens if I am not satisfied with the final output? +

Client satisfaction is our priority. Any revisions are resolved within 24-48 hours. We do not process the final balance payment until you are 100% satisfied with the outcome and ROI metrics.

06 Is my business data and raw footage secure with your team? +

Yes, we adhere to a strict Professional Ethical Code. All client data, raw footage, and brand strategies are kept 100% confidential and are never shared with third-party entities.
