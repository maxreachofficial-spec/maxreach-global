Fundamentals

1. What are the limitations of digital marketing?

Iski sabse badi limit hai **High Competition** aur Privacy Rules. Aaj kal har koi ads chala raha hai, isliye "Attention" mehnga ho gaya hai. Saath hi, agar internet down ho ya user tech-savvy na ho, toh aapki reach khatam.
The primary limitations include high market saturation and increasing privacy regulations (like GDPR). It also heavily relies on internet accessibility and requires constant technological updates to stay relevant.
Ads

2. What is pay-per-click (PPC) advertising?

Ye ek "Kiraye ki dukan" jaisa hai. Aap Google ya Meta ko paise dete ho apna ad dikhane ke liye, aur aapke paise tabhi kat-te hain jab koi user actually aapke ad par **Click** karta hai.
PPC is an online advertising model where advertisers pay a fee each time one of their ads is clicked. It’s essentially buying visits to your site rather than earning them organically.
SEO

3. What are Keywords?

Keywords wo "shabd" ya phrases hain jo log Google par type karte hain. SEO mein hum inhi words ko target karte hain taaki jab koi wo search kare, toh hamari site top par dikhe.
Keywords are the specific words or phrases that users type into search engines. They act as a bridge between what people are searching for and the content you are providing.
Strategy

4. Branding vs. Direct Marketing?

Branding ek **"Rishta"** hai (Trust banana), jabki Direct Marketing ek **"Sauda"** hai (Turant sale nikalna). Branding long-term hai, Direct Marketing short-term ROI ke liye hoti hai.
Branding focuses on building a long-term identity and emotional connection. Direct Marketing is performance-driven, aiming for immediate actions like a purchase or a sign-up.
UX

5. How to improve Conversion Rate (CRO)?

Iske liye humein user ka safar asan banana hota hai. Clear **Call-to-Action (CTA)**, fast loading speed, aur trust signals (reviews) add karke hum conversion badha sakte hain.
Improving CRO involves optimizing the user experience, using compelling CTAs, A/B testing headlines, and ensuring the website is fast and mobile-responsive.
Search

6. What is Search Engine Marketing (SEM)?

SEM ka matlab hai Search engines (Google) par **Paid Ads** ke zariye visibility badhana. Jab aap search results mein top par 'Ad' likha hua dekhte ho, wo SEM hai.
SEM is a digital marketing strategy used to increase the visibility of a website in search engine results pages (SERPs) through paid advertising.
Direct

7. What is Email Marketing?

Ye apne clients ke "In-box" mein direct pahunchne ka tarika hai. Isse hum updates, offers aur personalized content bhej kar purane customers ko wapas late hain.
Email Marketing is the act of sending commercial messages to a group of people via email to build loyalty, share news, or drive sales.
Analysis

8. Digital vs. Traditional Marketing?

Agar target audience badi hai aur budget kam, toh Digital best hai. Lekin agar local area mein sabko ek saath dikhana hai (jaise Mandi mein), toh Traditional kaam aati hai.
Use Digital when you need precise targeting and data tracking. Use Traditional when you want broad local awareness or are targeting a demographic offline.
Insights

9. Is Digital better than Traditional?

Digital isliye better hai kyunki ye **Measurable** hai. Aapko pata hota hai ki 1-1 rupaya kahan kharch hua. Traditional mein aap sirf andaza laga sakte ho.
Digital is often considered superior because of its cost-effectiveness, real-time data tracking, and the ability to engage in two-way communication.
Finance

10. How to calculate ROI?

Iska simple formula hai: **(Munafa - Kharcha) / Kharcha * 100**. Agar aapne 100 kharch karke 500 kamaye, toh aapka ROI 400% hua.
ROI is calculated by subtracting the cost from the total revenue, then dividing that by the cost of the campaign, multiplied by 100.
